  • amada (india) pvt. ltd.

    Comprehensive manufacturer of metalworking machinery

    The AMADA Group comprises about 91 companies. Our main businesses are the manufacture, sale, repair, maintenance, check, and inspection of metalworking machines and equipment.

    We, AMADA Group, provide a full range of solution, not only five major businesses which are Sheet metal machinery, Cutting machine, Grinding machine, Stamping press, and Micro welding, but also software, peripheral equipment, tooling, maintenance and service. As a comprehensive global manufacturer of metalworking machinery, AMADA contributes to the development of manufacturing considering what is most necessary from the customer's point of view.

  • AMADA established Amada (India) Pvt. Ltd. in April 2000, with its office in Mumbai and this is a wholly owned subsidiary of Amada Co. Ltd., Japan. In the present day, the company shifted base and has a functional Technical and Vocational Center in Bangalore. Further to provide prompt sales and after sales services to our customers, apart from Bangalore Technical Center, our sales and service engineers are also stationed in Bangalore, Chennai, Coimbatore, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Pune & Vadodara.

    To cater to the wide northern region we have recently inaugurated the North Satellite Center in Faridabad.
