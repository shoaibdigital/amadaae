Amada (India) Private Limited in association with Muse Creation a charity NGO of Japanese volunteers living in Bangalore, brings together the collective, creative talent of our Japanese community in Bangalore, to benefit various charities, reaching out to the socially and economically underprivileged sections of the society both in rural and urban India.

Amada (India) Private Limited in association with Muse Creation funded to three reliable charitable trusts

  • New Ark Mission - Home of Hope

    An organization whose mission is Running home for destitute & dying people who are picked off the streets of Bangalore and Provide medical treatment and long-term rehabilitation & a dignified place to end their days.

  • Jagruthi Trust

    A Non-governmental Child Care organization focusing on rescuing, rehabilitating and reintegrating needy children under “high risk behaviour” Operates 2 creches & 2 home care centre’s for children who are at risk/sexually exploited/abused or who have been infected/affected by HIV. It Conducts HIV awareness & education programs, drop-in health clinics, & outreach & service to commercially & sexually exploited people.

  • One Billion Literates Foundation (OBLF)

    An organization whose mission is to impart essential basic English & Computer skills to underprivileged children and the community around them by using the existing infrastructure of government schools
