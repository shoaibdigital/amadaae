    Achieve the Perfect Grind every time

    Automated, precise, high-speed grinding

    The TOGU -III has been created to assist the grinding process and to considerably reduce the loss in production time and will help every customer obtain the highest quality of grinding.

    Development Concept

    High-precision automated grinding designed specially for turret punch press tooling.

    Servo-controlled wet rotary grinding provides unsurpassed surface finish while significantly increasing tool life.

    Easy operation, experienced grinding operator is not required.

    Small footprint allows strategic shop placement.
