  • LC3015F1 Unveiling ceremony

    The LC3015 F1 NT with ASF3015, India’s first fully unmanned Laser cell was officially launched. The LC3015 F1 NT is a high speed, high accuracy Linear Laser, one of the worlds’ fastest. It features a new Amada tuned oscillator and a new cutting head to dramatically improve cut quality, stability with the highest processing speeds.

    The ASF3015 unmanned cell enables factories to achieve high mix, high volume, “lights out production”.

  • Launch of RGM2 series bending machine

    Building further on the already established reliability and accuracy of the RG series, Amada launched the RGM2 series Press Brakes. Apart from higher bending speeds, the RGM2 has Amada’s new CNC Control and One Touch Tool change system to reduce set up and change-over times, increasing flexibility and repeatability without depending on operator skill.
