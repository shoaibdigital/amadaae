  • Precision Sheet Metal Technology Fair

  • Technology Fair

    The "Precision Sheet Metal Technology Fair" is a skill contest designed to promote advanced sheet metal processing technologies, which was started in 1989 by the Amada School. We accept applications for products in 5 categories from May every year, and after careful screening, awarding ceremonies are held in next March.

    The judging board consists of the academic institute, industrial associations, visitors to the Amada Solution Center, and judges appointed separately. Aside from Gold, Silver, and Bronze prizes, additional prizes are also awarded from The Ministry of Health, Labor and Welfare, Minstry of Economy, Trade and Industry, Japan Vocational Ability Development Association, The Nikkan Kogyo Shimbun, Ltd., and Amada Co., Ltd.
