  • EG series

    High speed and high accuracy electric servo drive press brake

    An ultra-high precision, high-speed compact bending solution featuring an advanced and precise Dual Servo Power drive system

    • Automatic material thickness detection.
    • BI-S or BI-L automatic angle measurement system options
    • AMNC3i - 19" Multi touch screen 3D control
    • Thickness detection and force control system
    • Amada A Grip-M tool clamps

FEATURES

Dual Servo Power [DSP] Drive

  • Dual servo AC power drive system contains two AC servo motors - one motor for bending and the other for high speed approach/return.
  • Extremely fast approach, bending & return speed.
  • Highly accurate position detection and positioning.

L-Shift Back gauge

  • 5 axis back gauge speeds setup for complex parts.
  • High-Speed movement on all axes ensures that the gauge fingers are positioned as quickly as the operator positions the part.
  • L-Axes Servo Drive (L-Shift) handle bending of a wide variety of part shapes.

High Rigidity & Larger Open Height

  • High-rigidity frame allows for a larger open height and stroke.
  • Larger open height allows deep box bending& easier retraction of parts with large down flanges
