  • EM-ZR series

    Fully automatic punching solution machine

    Fusion of original technologies to achieve processing with high speed, productivity & grade

    EM-ZR series comes with the world’s unparalleled drive system, or AC servo direct twin drive and ZR turret, a major innovation since the development of Amada’s first numerically controlled turret punch press. Parts can be produced not only with high speed and productivity but also with less scratches. Further with ID tools and elimination of processing limits, the EM-ZR series can perform high-mix low-volume production continuously over 72 hours.

FEATURES

High Quality & High Speed Processing

  • High Speed Processing with scratch free bottom surface ZR turret and completely flat table.
  • Easy Programming – No track dead zones, free tool location

Stable High Quality Processing [Digital Quality management with ID tools]

  • Prevention of tool setup mistakes – ID tools
  • Stabilization of high quality processing – Tool condition management
  • Elimination of shimming time – Automatic die height adjustment

Automation of Tool Setup [Reduction of programming time & machine downtime]

  • Easy creation of nesting data – High tool storage capacity
  • Reduction of machine downtime – Automatic tool changing system
