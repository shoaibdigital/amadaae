  • About Us

    The AMADA Vocational Center is committed to work with customers and their employees in the Sheet Metal Industry with a view of imparting knowledge to the operators related to Safety, Machine Operation and Maintenance to make them completely competent.

  • Objective

    To overcome the shortage of skilled workers, we want to work together with our customers and their employees and give them the ability to constantly live up to the ever-greater technical requirements in the field of Sheet Metal Industry.

  • Values

    • Efficient & Productive use of Machine & Software.
    • Dedicated Facility & Faculty.
    • Interactive Off-line & On-line training
    • Focused on Process Know-How
    • Networking among customers.
    • Exposure to New tools and techniques.
    • Performance oriented certification.
    • Post training support.
